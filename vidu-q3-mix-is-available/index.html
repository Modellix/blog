Vidu Q3-Mix upgrades reference-based video generation with stronger camera control, character motion, and visual consistency. Now on Modellix — 50% off through June 28.

AI drama is having a moment. From short-form story clips to serialized animated content, creators are using video models to move faster from idea to scene. But making one good clip is not the same as building a workflow that can support ongoing production.

For teams building ads, interactive content, AI drama, e-commerce videos, or social clips, the challenge is no longer just generating a good-looking video. It is generating videos that stay consistent, scale across use cases, and fit into real production needs.

That is why we are excited to announce that Vidu Q3-Mix is now available on Modellix.

As part of the Vidu Q3 series, Vidu Q3-Mix brings a major upgrade to reference-based video generation. It is designed to support industrialized, scalable, and high-quality video production across interactive content, advertising, AI drama, and other creative scenarios.

What Vidu Q3-Mix Brings

Vidu Q3-Mix helps users generate dynamic video content from reference images, characters, products, or scenes. Instead of relying only on text prompts, users can start from a clear visual reference and guide the output more closely toward the intended subject, style, and scene.

According to Vidu’s product materials, Q3-Mix focuses on four areas:

  • Camera control: improved handling of camera movement, shot transitions, and framing
  • Character motion: more natural expressions, actions, emotional shifts, and interactions
  • Prompt following: better understanding and execution of complex prompts
  • Consistency: stronger consistency across characters, environments, props, clothing, and visual style

For production teams, these improvements can help reduce iteration costs and make AI-generated video more suitable for repeatable workflows.

Built for Real Content Production

Vidu Q3-Mix is suitable for:

  • Advertising: generate multiple video variations from product images or visual key art
  • E-commerce: turn product photos and scene visuals into dynamic product videos
  • AI drama and short-form series: support character motion tests, storyboard previews, and narrative clips
  • Interactive content and virtual characters: keep characters, scenes, and styles more stable across outputs
  • AI SaaS and developer workflows: integrate video generation into content tools, marketing tools, and prototype applications

  • manage usage and call logs in one place

This makes it easier for creators, developers, and enterprise users to evaluate Vidu Q3-Mix and bring it into broader content production or product workflows.
