Introducing Modellix: One API for All AI Media Generation

April 2, 2026
Modellix AI Product Launch API
Modellix Team Official
Summary

Modellix is officially live. Access 100+ AI media models — image, video, and audio — through a single API with transparent pricing and enterprise-grade reliability.

We’re thrilled to announce that Modellix is officially live.

Modellix is a Model-as-a-Service (MaaS) platform that brings the world’s top AI media models together under one roof. Instead of juggling multiple provider accounts, API keys, and incompatible parameter formats, you get a single API, a single dashboard, and a single billing view — for every model you need.

The Problem We Set Out to Solve

The AI media generation landscape is exploding. Dozens of providers now offer powerful models for creating images, videos, and audio — but integrating them is a different story. Each provider ships its own SDK, its own authentication scheme, and its own parameter structure. Scaling across providers means maintaining a patchwork of integrations, tracking costs in multiple dashboards, and debugging across disconnected logging systems.

We built Modellix to eliminate that friction entirely.

What Modellix Offers

One API, All Models

Modellix provides unified API access to 100+ AI models from 5 major providers spanning:

  • Text-to-Image — Generate images from prompts using Imagen 4.0, Nano Banana 2, Seedream 4.5, Qwen Image, Wan 2.6, Kling V2.1, and MiniMax Image.
  • Image-to-Image — Edit, expand, and transform existing images with Nano Banana 2 Edit, Kling Image Expansion, Seedream, Qwen Image Edit, and more.
  • Text-to-Video — Create videos from text descriptions using Veo 3.1, Seedance 1.5, Hailuo 2.3, Kling V2.6, Wan 2.6, and MiniMax T2V.
  • Image-to-Video — Animate still images into video with Veo 3.1, Seedance 1.5, Kling V2.6, Hailuo 2.3, and Wan 2.6 — with fine-grained control over motion, duration, and resolution.
  • Virtual Try-On — Realistic AI-powered clothing try-on with Kolors Virtual Try-On, supporting single garments and full outfit combinations.
  • Special Effects — Avatar animation, talking-head video, 212 preset video effects, image segmentation, and more from the Kling family.

All through one API key with flattened, consistent parameters — no nested inputs, no provider-specific quirks.

Transparent, Predictable Pricing

Every model’s pricing is published openly on our pricing page. Image generation starts as low as $0.004/image. Video generation starts at $0.0015/second. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices.

Alibaba’s model family deserves a special callout here — their pricing on Modellix is among the most competitive on the market. Wan 2.2 I2V Flash starts at just $0.0015/second, the lowest video generation price on the entire platform. Wanx 2.1 T2V Turbo and Wanx 2.1 I2V Turbo offer flat rates of $0.038/sec and $0.037/sec respectively — no resolution-based tiers, no surprise upcharges. For image generation, Qwen Image Plus comes in at $0.018/image with strong bilingual (Chinese/English) text rendering that most competing models struggle with. If you’re optimizing for cost without sacrificing quality, the Alibaba lineup on Modellix is hard to beat.

Full Observability

Every API call is logged with inputs, outputs, costs, and metadata — giving you complete traceability for debugging, auditing, and cost analysis. Real-time billing history provides a 360-degree view of your spending at any time.

Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure

Modellix is backed by a NASDAQ-listed company (JG) with over 15 years of enterprise infrastructure experience. We’re built for reliability, security, and scale from day one.

Multiple Ways to Use

Beyond the REST API, Modellix also offers:

  • CLI — Trigger models, manage tasks, and automate workflows from your terminal.
  • Agent Skill — Integrate Modellix seamlessly into AI agents and low-code workflows.
  • MCP Server — Search Modellix documentation directly from your MCP-compatible clients.
